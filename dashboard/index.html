Six tools, one historical record

Event Plan Optima turns a real, anonymized six-month Bengaluru Traffic Police export into five working tools: a single-event forecaster, a 7-day outlook with a week planner, a historical insights view, a published model-validation report, and a live triage + learning-loop console. Every number on every page traces back to the same dataset and the same two trained models — nothing here is recomputed differently from one screen to the next.
